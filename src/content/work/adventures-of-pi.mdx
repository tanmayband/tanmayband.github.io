## What?
A singleplayer puzzle platformer where you play as Pi, a character that lives on the number line. Solve math puzzles and fight number enemies using math-based abilities, like addition, subtraction, and integration and differentiation. The idea of the game is to make complex math concepts tangible and enjoyable.

## Gameplay
a "math mode" to manipulate the world using math powers
- circle shifting of origin
- differentiation tangent
- integration area under curve

## Development
- loved the fact that many seemingly complex math concepts can be interpreted as geometry, which works well for a game
- used procedural mesh to slice for area under curve
- implemented a slope calculator for the differentiation tangent, which works of any function curve including circles
- work was ongoing to bring in arithmetic abilities
